Meet the Artists

The People Behind the Chair

SV
Steve Vargas
Owner & Master Barber · 20+ Years

Steve has been shaping the Gaithersburg community one cut at a time since 2003. Known for precision fades and meticulous detail, he's built a loyal following that spans generations — clients who started as kids now bring their own children to the chair. A true craftsman who takes pride in every single line.

Precision Fades Classic Cuts Kids Cuts Line-ups
MR
Marcus Rivera
Senior Barber · 8 Years

Marcus brings a modern edge to a classic craft. Trained at the Maryland Barber Institute and specializing in high skin fades and contemporary restyling, his creative instincts and steady hand make him the go-to for clients looking to refresh — or completely reinvent — their look.

Skin Fades Restyling Beard Sculpting Textured Hair
TV
Terri Vargas
Licensed Stylist & Co-Owner

Terri is the other half of the Exclusive Cuts legacy. A licensed cosmetologist and co-owner, she brings expertise in women's styling, color treatments, and textured hair care. Her warmth and technical skill ensure every client — male or female — feels completely at home in the chair.

Women's Styling Color Textured Hair Blow-out

Our Story

About Our Shop

Welcome

Crafting Confidence, One Cut at a Time

Jimmy King Dominican Barbershop & Salon was founded on a simple idea: every client, regardless of hair type or texture, deserves a barber who takes the time to get it right. Jimmy trained in the Dominican tradition of precision barbering, where craftsmanship and personal connection matter as much as the finished cut.

Our shop stays intentionally intimate — small enough that you can talk through exactly what you want, and consistent enough that you'll see the same familiar faces every visit. We take pride in working confidently with every hair texture, from tight curls to straight, fine hair, and we never rush a client through the chair.

Whether it's a first-time visit, a squeezed-in appointment on a holiday, or a patient, gentle cut for a child who needs extra care, we treat every appointment with the same respect and attention to detail — because a great haircut should always feel personal.

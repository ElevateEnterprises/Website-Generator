Our Team

Meet the Barbers
GH
George Harrison
Master Barber & Owner

George has been behind the chair since the 1980s, carrying forward a tradition of precision cuts and old-school hospitality. He learned the craft from his father and has spent over four decades mastering the art of the straight razor shave and the perfect taper. His clients aren't customers — they're family.

Classic Cuts Straight Razor Tapers 40+ yrs
MT
Marcus Thompson
Senior Barber

Marcus brings 15 years of experience and a modern eye to the shop. Trained in both traditional and contemporary techniques, he specializes in skin fades, beard sculpting, and bold restyling that keeps clients looking fresh between visits. His clientele spans all ages and hair types.

Skin Fades Beard Sculpt Modern Styles 15 yrs
DC
David Cruz
Barber

David is the shop's specialist in kids' cuts, line-ups, and textured curl work. With 8 years behind the chair, he has a gentle, patient approach that makes even the most restless clients feel at ease. Parents love him because kids leave looking sharp and actually smile doing it.

Kids Cuts Line-ups Curl Work 8 yrs

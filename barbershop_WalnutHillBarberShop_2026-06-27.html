Master Barber · Owner
George has been wielding the scissors since the 1950s, making him one of the most experienced barbers in all of Maryland. Clients travel far and wide just to sit in his chair. His cuts are first class, his chair-side manner is second to none, and every visit feels like coming home. There is simply nobody quite like him.

A

Abraham "Ab"

Owner & Master Barber

Ab has built his reputation on precision and hygiene — clippers get cleaned after every single client, and every cut is unhurried. Regulars have followed him for years, drawn to his sharp customer service, clear communication, and consistently flawless fades. He's also famously kid-friendly, letting the little ones pick something to watch while he works.

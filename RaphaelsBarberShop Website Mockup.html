Our Story

About Our Shop

Welcome

More Than a Haircut—It's an Experience

Raphael's Barber Shop was founded by Ebrahim, whose commitment to precision and old-school craftsmanship laid the foundation for what the shop is today. Now, more than 15 years later, that same standard is carried on alongside his son Sonny, and a team of barbers who share the same respect for the trade.

We listen before we cut, we never rush a fade, and we treat every visit — from a first-time walk-in to a decades-long regular — with the same level of care. Clients don't just come back for the haircut; they come back because they know their barber and trust the shop.

More than a haircut, a visit to our shop is a few minutes of consistency in a busy week: a clean, welcoming space, honest pricing, and barbers who know your name.

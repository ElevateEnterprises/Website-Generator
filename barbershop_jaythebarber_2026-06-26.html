Our Team

Meet the Barbers

Skill. Passion. Precision.

J
Jay
Owner & Master Barber

With over a decade behind the chair, Jay built Classic Cuts from the ground up on one simple principle: every client deserves more than a haircut — they deserve an experience. His shop features ambient lighting, a fully stocked beer fridge, a vintage arcade console with thousands of pre-loaded games, and live instruments (Jay is an accomplished musician with a YouTube channel and local band). Come for the cut, stay for the vibe.

Skin Fades Classic Cuts Hot Towel Shaves 10+ Years
M
Marcus
Senior Barber

Marcus joined Classic Cuts after honing his craft in Boston's South End barbershop scene for six years. His specialty is flawless skin fades and bald tapers — clients drive from across New Hampshire to sit in his chair. Off the clock, you'll find him collecting vintage sneakers and playing guitar in Jay's basement studio sessions.

Skin Fades Bald Taper Beard Sculpting 6 Years Exp.
T
Tyler
Barber

Tyler is the newest addition to the Classic Cuts family and brings a sharp eye for modern styles and creative designs. He trained at the Paul Mitchell School in Manchester and quickly built a loyal following with his textured crops, hard parts, and geometric design work. His energy in the shop is infectious — clients leave with a great cut and an even better mood.

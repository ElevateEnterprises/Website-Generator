Our Team
The Hands Behind the Craft
Meet the Barbers
J
Jay
Master Barber & Owner
With over a decade behind the chair, Jay built Classic Cuts from the ground up with one mission: make every client leave looking and feeling their best. Known for his patient, detail-oriented approach and legendary conversation, Jay has turned first-timers into lifelong regulars. Off the clock, he's a musician with a YouTube channel and plays in a local band — ask him about it.
Skin Fades Classic Cuts Restyles Beard Design Kids Cuts
M
Marcus
Senior Barber
Marcus brings eight years of professional experience and a passion for precision to every cut. A graduate of the New England Barber Academy, he's built a loyal following for his flawless fades and razor-sharp line work. Marcus specializes in textured and curly hair, bringing out the best in every natural pattern. Expect technical mastery with a laid-back, welcoming energy.
Textured Hair High Fades Line-ups Curly Hair
D
Diego
Barber
Diego is the newest addition to the Classic Cuts family, bringing fresh creative energy and sharp technical skills from his training in Boston. He has a natural eye for modern styles and transformations, and his clients love him for his attentiveness and ability to translate ideas into reality. Whether it's a subtle clean-up or a bold restyle, Diego delivers every time.

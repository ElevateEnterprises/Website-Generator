Meet the Barbers
Skilled hands. Sharp eyes. Decades of combined experience.
JB
Jay
Owner & Master Barber
Jay has been shaping up Manchester for over a decade. Known for his precision fades, warm personality, and a shop that feels more like a hangout than a barbershop — complete with a custom arcade console, ambient lighting, and a beer fridge. He's a musician, a community figure, and the reason most clients never go anywhere else.
Classic Cuts Skin Fades Vintage Styles Beard Craft
MC
Marcus
Senior Barber
Marcus brings eight years of chair experience and a meticulous eye for detail. His specialties include high-contrast fades, full beard sculpts, and patient, thorough kids' cuts. Clients trust him to translate a vague idea into a sharp, clean execution every single time.
Modern Fades Beard Sculpting Kids Cuts Line-ups

The Barbers Behind The Chair

Decades of combined experience, three distinct styles, one standard of precision.

N

Nancy

Master Barber & Owner

With over a decade behind the chair, Nancy built My Barber Shop's reputation one flawless fade at a time. She takes her time with every guest, navigating tricky cowlicks and stubborn textures with ease, and is known for cuts that look "straight out of a magazine" without ever rushing the process.

Precision Fades Women's Cuts Complex Styling
J

Jessi

Senior Barber

Jessi joined the shop as the newest face behind the chair and quickly became a client favorite for her precision and personality. She works confidently across men's and women's hair, often shaping a cut entirely freehand for the cleanest possible finish.

Modern Styles Freehand Shaping Men's & Women's Cuts
M

Marcus

Barber

Marcus rounds out the team with a sharp eye for detail and a calm, easygoing chair-side manner. From skin fades to full beard sculpting, he focuses on clean lines and finishes every visit with a hot towel touch that regulars swear by.

Skin Fades Beard Sculpting Hot Towel Finish

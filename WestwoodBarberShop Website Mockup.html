Our Story

About Our Shop

Welcome

Where Style Meets Precision

Westwood Barber Shop has been a fixture of the Westbard neighborhood, built on a simple promise: a great haircut at a fair price, delivered by people who genuinely enjoy the work. Customers across the DMV tell us the same thing — this is one of the friendliest chairs around, and it shows in how long our regulars stick with us.

We're a family-friendly shop first. Parents bringing in kids for their very first haircut will find iPads on hand to keep little ones calm and entertained while our barbers work — a small touch that makes a big difference on a busy Saturday. It's part of why so many families make us their regular stop.

Every barber on our team is trained to the same standard, so it truly doesn't matter which chair you sit in — you're getting a careful, precise cut every time. We've got clients who have been coming back every six to eight weeks for twenty-five years, and we take that kind of loyalty seriously. More than a haircut, a visit here is honest service, a fair price under $30, and barbers who are genuinely glad to see you.

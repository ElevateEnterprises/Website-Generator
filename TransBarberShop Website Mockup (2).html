Our Story

About Our Shop

Get to know the people, principles, and passion behind Tran's Barber Shop.

Since Day One

Crafting Confidence, One Cut at a Time

For years, Tran's Barber Shop has been a fixture of the Bethesda community — a place where walk-ins are welcomed like regulars and every client leaves the chair looking sharper than when they sat down.

Our team takes pride in getting the details right: matching a fade to your hair texture, shaping a beard to your bone structure, and finishing every visit with a traditional hot towel touch. It's not just a haircut here — it's a standard we hold ourselves to on every single client, every single day.

Whether you've been coming in for years or you're stopping by for the first time, our goal is the same: precision work, honest conversation, and a shop that feels like part of the neighborhood.

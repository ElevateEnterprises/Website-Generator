The Craftsmen Behind the Chair

Our Team

Meet the Barbers
Skilled hands, genuine people, and a shared passion for the craft.
J
Jay
Master Barber & Owner
Jay has spent over a decade sharpening his craft and building relationships one cut at a time. Known for his patience, precision, and warm personality, he's turned his chair into a destination — not just for a haircut but for an experience. The shop features ambient lighting, a beer fridge, and an arcade with thousands of preloaded games. Jay is also a musician with his own YouTube channel and performs live in a band.
Classic FadesHot Towel ShavesTapersSkin Fades
M
Marcus
Senior Barber
Marcus brings sharp technique and an eye for detail that keeps clients coming back. He specializes in clean skin fades and sculptural beard work, and has built a loyal following in the Manchester community over five years behind the chair. His calm energy makes him a favorite among first-time clients and long-time regulars alike.

Dre
Barber
Dre joined the team after graduating from the New England Barber Academy and quickly made a name for himself working with kids and clients with textured hair. His relaxed demeanor puts even the most nervous young clients at ease. He's also developing serious freehand design skills and specializes in natural and curly hair patterns.

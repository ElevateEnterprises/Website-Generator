Crafting Confidence, One Cut at a Time

More Than a Haircut — It's an Experience

Wilson Barber opened its doors in Takoma Park with a simple promise: every client leaves looking sharper and feeling more confident than when they walked in. We built this shop on precision technique, honest conversation, and respect for the craft of barbering.

Every haircut, fade, and shave is treated as a craft, not a routine. Our barbers take the time to understand your hair, your face shape, and your lifestyle before the clippers ever touch your head.

We keep our tools sharp, our chairs clean, and our standards high — because a great barbershop is judged by consistency, visit after visit.

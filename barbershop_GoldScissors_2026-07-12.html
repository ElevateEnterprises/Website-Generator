OUR TEAM

The Craftsmen

Skilled Hands, Precise Results

A

Antonio "Ace" Rivera

Owner & Master Barber

Ace opened Gold Scissors on South Broadway with one goal: bring old-school barbershop craftsmanship back to Baltimore. Over 12 years behind the chair, he's built a reputation for razor-sharp lines and fades that hold their shape for weeks. Regulars book him for milestone cuts and can't-miss occasions.

D

Deja Coleman

Senior Barber

Deja is the shop's go-to for intricate fade designs and full transformations. She trained in both barbering and freehand hair art, and clients travel across the city specifically for her detail work. Patient, precise, and always up on the latest styles.

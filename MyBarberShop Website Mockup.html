Our Services

Every service at My Barber Shop is delivered with precision, patience, and premium tools.

01

Haircuts

Precision haircuts tailored to your style, face shape, and personal preferences for a fresh, clean look. Choose from classic cuts, buzz cuts, scissor work, and modern styling — all customized to you.

02

Fade Haircuts

Expertly blended low, mid, high, taper, and skin fades with smooth transitions and sharp finishes. Our barbers specialize in seamless blends including burst, drop, and bald fades.

03

Beard Trims & Shaping

Professional beard trimming and contouring to keep your facial hair clean, balanced, and well-defined. Includes precision lineups and edge work for a sharp, polished look.

04

Straight Razor Shaves

A traditional hot towel straight razor shave that delivers an exceptionally close, smooth, and refreshing finish. A timeless service rooted in old-school barbering technique.

05

Hair & Beard Combo

The complete grooming experience, combining a haircut and beard service for a polished, coordinated appearance — our most popular full-service booking.

Our Story

About Our Shop

Crafting Confidence, One Cut at a Time.

More Than a Haircut—It's an Experience

Where Style Meets Precision

My Barber Shop was built on a simple idea: every client deserves a haircut that's precise, a shave that's relaxing, and a shop that feels like it's theirs. Located in the heart of Bethesda, Maryland, we've spent years earning the trust of a loyal, local clientele — one appointment at a time.

Our barbers bring real experience to the chair, with some team members serving clients for over a decade. That kind of continuity means we know your fade, your part, and exactly how you like your lineup — without you having to explain it twice.

We keep the shop clean, the tools sharp, and the atmosphere welcoming, because a great haircut should be part of a great experience from the moment you walk in to the moment you check yourself out in the mirror on the way out.
